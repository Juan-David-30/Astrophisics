Requirements

I'll take the requirements of the University of Oxford as reference, because in my case is the university where I'd like to study astrophysics.


Knowing that the best choice to study astrophysics is go abroad we should have a great level of english, enough to understand the technical language, so the ideal is have a C1 or at least a B2 level, although is better to have the C1 certified.


However as we alredy said we must have an undergraduate in maths, physics or astronomy

Else we must have solid bases in maths and physics, that includes knowledge in:

  • Arithmetic

Admission

Firstly we must to apply to the university and subject, then we must to present the exam of admition, in the case of the University of Oxford there is an exam that we must present depending of the program that we want to apply for, in the case of physics, maths, engineering, and this kind of careers the exam that we must to apply is the PAT (Physics Aptitue Text), that is an exam where all our knowledge about maths and physics will be valued.


Once we've presented the exam, one proccess of selection will be executed, if we pass this selection proccess we'll be interviewed. In case of being selected after this proccess we can say that we are in


Note: Knowing that we are foreign students we must to present the certified of our English level as part of our admission proccess
